Black Lives Matter.

One place you could vote to have the profits donated to is the George Floyd's Memorial fund. On May 25, 2020, George Floyd, a 46-year-old black man, died after Derek Chauvin, a white police officer, knelt on his neck for almost nine minutes while he was handcuffed face down in the street. Many outcries for justice followed his death on social media and in protest around the world. The video on the right from The New York Times is one definitely worth watching.

Black Lives Matter: Say their names.

Police brutality is a terrible reality. All over the USA, African Americans fear being killed by those who enforce laws because of the color of their skin. We won't let them die in vain. Say their names.
